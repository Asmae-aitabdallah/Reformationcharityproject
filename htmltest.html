REFORMATION CHARITY RAMADAN CAMPAIGN 2025

This Ramadan, we're on a mission to support families in need in national and international communities by distributing essential packages, providing gifts and hosting festive parties. Our campaign seeks to offer assistance and relief to individuals and families facing hardship, especially in these challenging economic times.

International Projects

Reformation Charity is exci​​ted to have partnered with World Care Foundation on a life-changing mission to support communities in need including Syrian Refugees. We will be undertaking two critical projects: deployments and building our very first orphanage.

Deployments

Volunteers will be on the ground to directly deliver essential supplies and offer hands-on support to Syrian Refugees.

Through fundraising, your donations will allow us to: 

  • Engage in hands-on activities to help refugees
  • Packaging and distributing food and hygiene boxes to refugee camps
  • Visit and assist vulnerable families where you will have the opportunity to listen to their stories and hardships firsthand
  • Organise activities for orphaned and refugee children, providing emotional and social care

Building an orphanage

With your donations, we are laying the foundation of building our first orphanage, a safe haven where orphaned children can find safety and education. This orphanage will be more than just a shelter—it will be a place where children can heal, grow, and build a future filled with hope.

National Projects

The Grocery Drive

This Ramadan, join us in fundraising money for families in London who live under the poverty line. Your contributions will enable us to provide essential items, including food and toiletries, bringing relief and comfort to those going through difficulties. Some of these items include:

  • Lentils, Milk, Rice, Pasta, Cereal, Toilet paper, Fresh fruit and vegetables.

Eid Gifting 

Let's extend our hands and hearts to bring joy to children. With your generous donations, we will be purchasing and gift wrapping Eid gifts, bringing excitement and memories on a day of celebration. Your contribution, no matter the size, will make a tangible difference in their lives and help us create a truly blessed and memorable Ramadan and Eid for all.

Eid Celebration  

The Family Eid Party aims to bring happiness and a sense of belonging to families during culturally significant times of the year. Many of these families face financial and social challenges that prevent them from celebrating these occasions. These parties provide an opportunity for families to come together, enjoy a festive meal, participate in fun activities, and receive gifts for their children, fostering a sense of community and celebration.

Renovation Project 

The Renovation Initiative builds on our ongoing efforts to assist local families in need by improving living conditions that impact their health and well-being, particularly for households with children. Through essential upgrades like replacing damaged furniture and repainting, we aim to create safer, more comfortable, and functional homes that promote a healthier environment.

 The focus will be on:

  • Improving bedrooms, ensuring children have safe and comfortable sleeping arrangements
  • Creating organised and safe spaces for play and study
  • Enhancing common areas for family use

How it will work:

Volunteers are provided with the chance to join us to fundraise in order to package and distribute essential items both locally (The Grocery Drive) and internationally (via deployment). Volunteers also have the opportunity to gift wrap Eid gifts and renovate homes for the families we help.

Every donation and every effort counts, and together, we are creating a positive impact in our community. We truly appreciate your involvement and dedication to helping those who are facing difficult times. Thank you for being a part of this important cause!
